The problem

The financing question kills more deals than the price does.

The fix is not better salespeople. The fix is putting the answer where the question lives.

A buyer falls in love with a 62-foot listing on your site. They ask what the monthly payment looks like. Your team estimates. The buyer leaves to do the math elsewhere. By the time they figure it out, they have looked at three other boats.

Most brokerages send buyers to a generic calculator or a lender pitch. Both options break the conversation. Both options route the lead away from you.

The EasyFund calculator answers the payment question on your site. Buyers stay engaged. When they continue to pre-qualification on YachtWay, the lead is attributed to you automatically. When the loan closes, you earn a referral fee.

About EasyFund

EasyFund connects buyers with matching lenders through a proprietary application process based on their credit profile. YachtWay is a technology platform. YachtWay is not a lender and does not offer financial advice.

Every loan is underwritten by a partner lender in the EasyFund network. Final approval, rates, and terms are determined by the lender.
